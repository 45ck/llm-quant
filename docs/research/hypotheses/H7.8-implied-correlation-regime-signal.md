## Statement

The CBOE 3-Month Implied Correlation Index (COR3M) measures the implied
correlation among S&P 500 constituents derived from options prices.
When the COR3M oscillator (deviation from its 60-day mean) crosses
negative while COR3M is still above 30 (declining from elevated
levels), this signals a transition from systemic fear to stock-picking
environment — go long SPY. Expected Sharpe 0.40-0.70 with MaxDD < 15%.

## Mechanism

Implied correlation captures the market's expectation of how correlated
S&P 500 stocks will move:

1. **High correlation = systemic risk**: When COR3M is elevated (>50),
   the market is pricing all stocks to move together — a "risk-on/
   risk-off" regime driven by macro factors. This coincides with
   stress periods where diversification fails.
2. **Declining from high = fear resolution**: When COR3M begins
   declining from elevated levels (oscillator turns negative while
   level > 30), it signals the transition from systemic fear to
   idiosyncratic risk. This transition coincides with market
   recovery — stocks begin differentiating again as the crisis abates.
3. **COR3M > 30 floor**: The level requirement (>30) ensures we're
   detecting declines from elevated correlation, not normal
   fluctuations at low levels. Below 30, correlation changes are
   noise within the normal dispersion regime.
4. **Options-implied forward-looking signal**: Unlike realized
   correlation (backward-looking), COR3M embeds the market's FORWARD
   expectation. When options traders reduce correlation bets, it
   reflects genuine reassessment of systemic risk, not just trailing
   price action.

## Measurement

- signal: "COR3M oscillator = COR3M - SMA(COR3M, 60d)"
- entry_condition: "Oscillator crosses below 0 AND COR3M current level > 30"
- entry: "Long SPY on close of signal day"
- exit: "COR3M drops below 20 (full dispersion regime) OR oscillator > +1 std (correlation re-spiking) OR 40 trading days held"
- position_sizing: "2% NAV per trade (Track A)"

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |

| oscillator_mean | 60-day SMA of COR3M | 3-month normalization |
| level_floor | 30 | Minimum correlation level for signal relevance |
| exit_level | 20 | Full dispersion = signal exhausted |
| exit_reversion | oscillator > +1 std | Correlation re-spiking = regime reversal |
| max_hold | 40 trading days | ~2 months — regime transitions are slow |

## Falsification Criteria

1. COR3M oscillator crossings have no forward predictive power for SPY returns (p > 0.05)
2. Simple VIX timing outperforms COR3M timing — correlation adds no incremental value over vol
3. Shuffled COR3M signal p-value > 0.05
4. Entry without the level > 30 floor performs equally well — the floor condition is unnecessary
5. Realized correlation (backward-looking) outperforms implied correlation — forward-looking aspect adds nothing
6. DSR < 0.95
