# Hypothesis H11.2: Overnight Return Decomposition Alpha

## Statement

Buying SPY and QQQ at the close and selling at the next open (capturing overnight
returns only) generates positive alpha because the equity risk premium accrues
predominantly overnight. Position size is scaled inversely to trailing 5-day
overnight volatility to reduce exposure during volatile overnight sessions.
Expected Sharpe > 0.70 with MaxDD < 15%.

## Mechanism

Cliff, Cooper, and Gulen (2008) documented that equity returns accrue almost
entirely overnight (close-to-open), not intraday (open-to-close). The mechanism
is structural:

1. **Information asymmetry resolution**: Material news (earnings, macro data,
   geopolitical events) is released outside market hours. The overnight return
   compensates holders for bearing this unhedgeable information risk.

2. **Institutional order flow**: Large institutional orders (pension contributions,
   index rebalancing, corporate buybacks) execute via MOC (market-on-close) orders,
   creating buying pressure at close that resolves overnight.

3. **Short-seller pattern**: Short sellers tend to cover at close (avoiding
   overnight margin risk) and re-establish positions at open, creating
   systematic close-up / open-down microstructure.

The vol-scaling (inverse of 5-day overnight vol) is the key risk management
innovation: it reduces position size automatically when overnight returns
become volatile (typically during earnings seasons and macro events), improving
the Sharpe ratio by ~30-50% (per Moreira-Muir 2017 vol-managed portfolios).

**WARNING: Transaction cost sensitivity.** The strategy trades twice daily
(buy at close, sell at open). At 2-4 bps per round trip, costs of 500-1000 bps
annually will erode a significant portion of gross alpha. The strategy MUST
survive a 2x cost stress test.

2. Strategy Sharpe < 0.40 after 2x transaction cost stress test (cost-killed)
3. Vol-scaling does NOT improve Sharpe by > 15% vs. unscaled version (scaling adds no value)
4. SPY overnight returns are not significantly positive (sign test p > 0.05)
5. Strategy correlation to SPY buy-and-hold > 0.60 (capturing beta, not overnight-specific alpha)
6. DSR < 0.95
