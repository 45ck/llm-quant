# Hypothesis H3.5: Skip-Month Cross-Asset TSMOM (Novy-Marx Refinement)

## Statement

A 12-month time-series momentum strategy that SKIPS the most recent month
(uses month t-12 to t-1, excluding t-1 to t) across 10 cross-asset ETFs
generates Sharpe > 0.80 with MaxDD < 15%. The skip-month eliminates
short-term mean-reversion contamination that degrades standard TSMOM.
Vol-scaling applied per Barroso-Santa-Clara (2015).

## Mechanism

Novy-Marx (2012, JFE) demonstrated that intermediate-horizon momentum
(7-12 months) is stronger and more robust than short-term momentum, and
that skipping the most recent month significantly improves performance.
The mechanism: the most recent month contains short-term mean-reversion
(Jegadeesh 1990) that partially offsets the momentum signal. By excluding
month t-1 to t, we isolate the pure trend component.

This is NOT the same as H3.1 (which blended 21d/63d/252d signals without
skip-month) or H3.3 (majority vote without skip-month). The skip-month
is a specific, literature-motivated refinement that addresses a known
contamination source.

Barroso-Santa-Clara (2015) showed vol-scaling improves momentum Sharpe
by ~50% and dramatically reduces crash risk. Combined with skip-month,
we get: (1) cleaner momentum signal from skip, (2) crash protection
from vol-scaling. This combination has not been tested in our pipeline.

## Measurement

- signal: "For each asset: return from t-252 to t-21 (skip most recent 21 trading days). Vol-scaled: signal / realized_vol(63d)."
- entry: "Long when vol-scaled signal > 0. Short when vol-scaled signal < 0."
- exit: "Signal reversal (crosses zero)."
- rebalance: "Monthly (every 21 trading days)"
- universe: "SPY, QQQ, EFA, EEM, TLT, IEF, GLD, USO, EURUSD=X, USDJPY=X"
- position_sizing: "Vol-parity: target 10% annualized portfolio vol. Per-asset weight = (vol_target / N) / asset_vol."

## Falsification Criteria

1. Skip-month does NOT improve Sharpe by > 10% vs. no-skip variant (skip is noise)
2. Shuffled signal p-value > 0.05 (no predictive timing)
3. 2022 drawdown > 15% (fails crisis diversification thesis)
4. Inverted signal produces flat (not negative) returns — signal not directional
5. Performance only in equities — no commodity/FX contribution
6. DSR < 0.95 after multiple-testing correction
