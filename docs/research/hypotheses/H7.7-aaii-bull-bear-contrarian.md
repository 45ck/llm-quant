## Statement

When the AAII bull-bear spread (% bullish minus % bearish) drops below
-20 (extreme retail pessimism), buy SPY and hold for 20 trading days.
The AAII sentiment survey is a well-documented contrarian indicator —
extreme pessimism historically precedes above-average returns. Expected
Sharpe 0.40-0.60 standalone (very low N of ~3-5 signals per year).

## Mechanism

The AAII Investor Sentiment Survey, conducted weekly since 1987, polls
individual investors on their market outlook. At extremes:

1. **Retail as contrarian indicator**: Individual investors are
   systematically wrong at extremes. The AAII survey captures retail
   sentiment directly — when bull-bear < -20, retail is maximally
   bearish. Academic literature (Fisher & Statman 2000, Clarke &
   Statman 1998) confirms that extreme AAII bearishness precedes
   above-average 1-3 month forward returns.
2. **Positioning exhaustion**: When the survey shows < 20% bulls, most
   retail investors have already reduced equity exposure. Further
   selling requires institutional participation, which tends to be
   more rational and mean-reverting. The exhaustion of retail selling
   creates a floor for prices.
3. **Anchoring and recency bias**: AAII respondents extrapolate recent
   returns into sentiment. After drawdowns, sentiment overshoots to
   the downside because respondents anchor to recent losses and
   project them forward — even though equity drawdowns are typically
   mean-reverting over 1-3 month horizons.
4. **20-day hold period**: Academic studies show the contrarian signal
   is strongest over 1-3 month horizons (20-60 trading days). The
   20-day hold captures the initial mean-reversion wave.

## Falsification Criteria

1. Forward 20-day SPY returns after bull-bear < -20 are not significantly positive (t-test p > 0.10 given low N)
2. Random entry days produce comparable returns — AAII timing has no value
3. Bullish extremes (bull-bear > +30) do not predict below-average returns — one-sided signal
4. Post-2015 effect has disappeared (social media changed sentiment dynamics)
5. VIX < 20 conditioning eliminates the effect — it's just buying low-VIX dips
6. Correlation to P/C ratio contrarian (H7.6) > 0.50 — redundant signal
