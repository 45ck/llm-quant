## Scan Results: Bug Discovered in O3-v2

The initial O3-v2 scan showing Sharpe=1.34 was a BUG: AssetRotationStrategy reads `lookback_days` (not `lookback_period`), so the scan was using default 60-day lookback. Additionally, `symbols_list` param was not set, so the strategy used default "SPY,TLT,GLD" instead of "GLD,USO,DJP". The strategy was essentially holding GLD alone.

Correct O3-v2 sweep (with `lookback_days` and `symbols_list`):
- lb=30 rank_by=sharpe: Sharpe=0.779, MaxDD=27.49% — MaxDD too high
- lb=45 tk=2: Sharpe=0.761, MaxDD=23.49% — MaxDD too high
- Best case (w=0.50): MaxDD=14.61%, Sharpe=0.582 — DSR=0.87, fails

**Conclusion:** Commodity rotation with GLD/USO/DJP cannot clear both DSR≥0.95 AND MaxDD<15% without a VIX regime overlay. Deferring O3-v2 until AssetRotationStrategy supports regime filtering.

## New Discoveries: GLD-SLV Pairs Ratio (M6)

**GLD-SLV Bollinger Band mean-reversion** — gold/silver ratio as a pairs trade.

Top results from parameter scan (bb_window × bb_std):
| Parameters | Sharpe | MaxDD | DSR | Trades |
|-----------|--------|-------|-----|--------|
| bb=60 std=2.0 | 1.278 | 11.60% | 0.9917 | 40 |
| bb=20 std=2.5 | 1.175 | 11.60% | 0.9845 | 38 |
| bb=30 std=1.5 | 0.986 | 14.20% | 0.9687 | 89 |
| bb=40 std=2.5 | 0.905 | 10.46% | 0.9490 | 29 |

**Selected for full lifecycle: bb=60 std=2.0**
- Theoretical justification: 60-day rolling window captures 3-month commodity cycles
- 2.0 sigma is the standard Bollinger Band threshold for mean-reversion strategies
- 40 trades is sufficient for CPCV folds
- MaxDD=11.60% — comfortable 3.4% margin below 15% gate

**Economic mechanism:** Silver (SLV) tends to follow gold (GLD) with higher beta (silver has both monetary and industrial demand). The GLD/SLV ratio has historically mean-reverted over weeks to months. When SLV underperforms GLD relative to historical ratio (ratio > upper band), SLV is cheap relative to gold → long SLV; when SLV outperforms (ratio < lower band) → long GLD.
