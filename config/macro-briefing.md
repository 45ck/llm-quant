## Key Themes

1. **AI investment boom** — US private AI funding hit $109B in 2024 (+30% YoY). 78% of organizations now use AI. This is the primary growth engine and a structural tailwind for tech/semiconductors.
2. **Monetary easing cycle** — Central banks expected to deliver modest rate cuts in 2026 as inflation recedes toward targets. Supports duration and rate-sensitive assets.
3. **Trade friction easing** — US eased some tariffs in early 2026. Reduced trade war premium, but policy uncertainty remains high with US midterms in November.
4. **Equity valuation stretch** — After 2025's rally, valuations are high. Analysts expect single-digit to low-double-digit returns — not a repeat of 2025.
5. **Green energy transition** — Renewables growing 15-20% annually. Infrastructure and clean energy spending rising under policy commitments.

## Key Risks

1. **Debt crisis** — High sovereign and corporate debt levels. A fiscal shock or sudden repricing of debt risk could trigger a hard landing.
2. **Geopolitical escalation** — Ukraine war ongoing, China-Taiwan tensions, Middle East instability. Any escalation could spike VIX and oil.
3. **Inflation stickiness** — If inflation doesn't continue easing, rate cuts get delayed or reversed. Bad for duration and growth.
4. **AI bubble risk** — If AI productivity gains don't materialize, the tech investment narrative unwinds. Concentrated exposure risk.
5. **Climate catastrophe** — Insurance losses exceeded $100B in 2025. Major climate events can disrupt supply chains and commodity prices.

## Asset Class Outlook

### Equities
- **US (SPY, QQQ, IWM):** Positive but moderating. AI tailwind for tech. Elevated valuations limit upside. Expect 8-12% total return.
- **International (EFA, VWO):** Broadening recovery. Non-US equities outperformed in 2025's rally. Europe moderate, Asia strong.
- **Sectors:** Tech/AI continues leading. Rotate toward beneficiaries of rate cuts (real estate, utilities) if easing materializes.

### Fixed Income
- **Long duration (TLT):** Benefits from rate cut cycle, but 17-year duration means significant rate sensitivity. Position carefully.
- **Aggregate (AGG):** Steady income in easing environment. Lower risk than TLT.
- **TIPS (TIP):** Hedge if inflation proves sticky. Watch breakevens.

### Commodities
- **Gold (GLD):** Geopolitical hedge. Supported by central bank buying and uncertainty premium.
- **Oil (USO):** Range-bound around $75. Upside risk from geopolitical escalation; downside from demand slowdown.
- **Broad (DBC):** Neutral. No strong commodity super-cycle signal.

### Crypto
- **BTC, ETH:** Volatile, regime-sensitive. Benefits from risk-on and liquidity expansion. Apply strict position limits (5% max).

### Forex
- **USD (UUP):** Mild weakening bias if Fed cuts rates. Watch relative rate differentials.
- **DXY basket:** Broadening global growth typically weakens dollar.
