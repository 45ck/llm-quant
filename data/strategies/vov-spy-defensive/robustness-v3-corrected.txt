## Verdict: HYPOTHESIS FALSIFIED
The correctly implemented VoV strategy produces 30 trades but with very weak Sharpe (0.211).
The VoV signal at 80th percentile triggers defensive exits too frequently during the 2022-2024
period, causing the strategy to miss significant SPY recoveries (2023-2024 bull run).

The degenerate prior results (Sharpe=0.539, 10 trades) were due to bug #3 — the strategy was
essentially near-constant SPY long (regime detection never triggered).

With correct implementation, VoV as a standalone SPY timing signal does NOT add value over
buy-and-hold SPY on this 5-year window (2021-2026).
